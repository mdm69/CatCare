Fostering from Shelter

Fostering is different with state county shelters, however calling a local shelter will let you know if they do any foster programs for kittens. Foster programs would mean that as the parent for the cats you choose to foster, you will monitor and support the mother cat in anyway that you can till they are of age ot be adopted. Or if the shelter allows, that you can adopt all the cats that you are currently fostering. With most foster programs, you will be provided any bedding, food, formula, toys and more for your foster kittens. I would only recommend fostering if you have a lot of time in your day and love to see kittens grow up. It is difficult but very rewarding and might fall in love with some.
