TNR Programs

For any stray it is important to stop a community of cats from growing. Cats on the street have a difficult life and usually cannot survive any longer then 2-4 years without any help. With the few who do feed and care for stray cats outside, it is important to spay or neuter any cat that we see. If the cat has a clip on their tip off on the right ear then most likely the cat has already been spay or neuter. Please do call your local shelter to help you with capturing outdoor cats and see what times are available to bring in any strays that you have captured.
